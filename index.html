MAX Daylight
  • iOS

The ultimate sunset app and more

I wrote this app to help me get home before dark while riding my bicycle. You can see at a glance how much time until sunset, and set notifications for when you are running out of sunlight. Your loved ones will no longer worry or complain.

Then I added everything solar but the kitchen sink, just for fun. Not only for your current location, you can monitor sunrise, noon, and sunset anywhere on Earth. You can compare locations, make movies, and generate reports comparing a location you select to places around the globe.

MirrorDay!
  • iOS

What if time traveled backwards?

Travel back in time every day from your Mirror Day, which can be your birthday, high school graduation, wedding anniversary, or any other fixed point in time.

Every day the reflection of today in your Mirror Day moves back another day, and we show you who was in political office, who was alive, who was deceased, and who would be born in the future. The persons are shown in order of the length of their Wikipedia article.

Get a notification once a day on weekdays, weekends, or both to check out a new person of historical importance.

Tap a picture tile and get a short biography, a big picture, timelines, and a link to the corresponding Wikipedia page.

Share your Mirror Day reflection with friends via text, email, Facebook, Twitter, you name it.

Great for history buffs seeking a personal connection to the lives of the people who made history.
