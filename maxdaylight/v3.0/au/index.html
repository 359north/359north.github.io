What's new in Version 3.0?

Version 3.0 is super improved over Version 2.1. Lunar data, 14 new graphic data panels, and 100+ enhancements! 3X more power efficient than Version 2.1.

Scroll down to see each of the 14 new data panels. You can tap any panel and access additional features.

Sun Panel


Displays sunrise, noon, sunset, length of day and changes from previous day. Tap to explore at different dates and times.

Solar Elevation Panel


Displays solar elevation and azimuth. Tap to explore at different dates and times.

Location Panel


Displays location, elevation and time zone information. Tap to view map and save more locations.

Distance Panel


Displays distance to current location, equator, and nearest pole. Tap to view map and save more locations.

Twilight Panel


Displays twilight times. Tap to explore at different dates and times.

Equinox Panel


Displays day length information for equinoxes and solstices, along with current day. Tap to explore at different dates and times.

Equation of Time Panel


Displays information about the Equation of Time, for sun dial enthusiasts. Tap to explore at different dates and times.

Comparison Panel


Compares latitude, day length and solar elevation for two locations. Tap to explore at different dates and times.

Moon Panel


Displays moonrise, moonset, lunar phase and illumination. Tap to explore at different dates and times.

Moon Elevation Panel


Displays lunar elevation and azimuth. Tap to explore at different dates and times.

Movie and Report Panel


Displays buttons to make a movie or generate a report. Tap to create and view movie or report.

Notifications Panel


Shows status of notifications you have set. Tap to manage your notifications.

Settings Panel


Displays buttons to manage Settings, including Options, Permissions, Rate and Review, and App Info, Privacy and Support. Tap to select options, review permissions, rate and review this app, and get app version and other information.

Statistics Panel


Displays data about your usage of the app. Tap to see how this data is kept private.
